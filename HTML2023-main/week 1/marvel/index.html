The X-Men

The X-Men are a superhero team. They fight to protect all mankind.

Team Members

Wolverine

Logan is a grumpy superhero. He has claws. He heals fast.

Jean Grey

Jean has cool mental powers. She died once, but she got better.

The School

The X-Men live at the School for Gifted Youngsters. It is in upstate NY.

At this school, young people learn to control their mutant powers while taking regular classes.

The world fears mutants. The X-Men like to stay at their school, hidden from the public.
